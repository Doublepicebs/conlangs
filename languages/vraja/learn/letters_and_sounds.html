Vraja

Letters and sounds




Letters

Vraja uses 16 letters, which are:
a /a/   d /d/   e /e/   f /f/   g /ɣ/   i /ɪ/   j /j/   k /k/   l /l/   m /n/   o /o/   p /p/   r /r/   s /s/   t /t/   v /v/  

Combinations and variations of letters can make different sounds:
sh /ʃ/   è, eh /ɛ/   th /θ/

Long vowels also exists in Vraja. ī, ō, ā.

NOTES

• The r is trilled, like Spanish.
• The g is pronounced like g in Spanish fuego, r in French mère or Arabic غني.
